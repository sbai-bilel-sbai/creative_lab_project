About Anime Dallas

With so many great audio recording and animation studios around the state, it’s no secret that Texas is anime strong! Anime Dallas is a fan-centric anime convention that celebrates everything the robust Texas anime community has to offer. Anime Dallas is for you – the anime fan! Our convention not only offers a chance to visit one-on-one with many of your favorite anime voice actors that call Texas home, but also the opportunity to experience fun vendors and amazing artists, great panels and exciting entertainment acts, impressive cosplay, and more – all at a price that won’t break the bank. Join our community today!
